About me

Hi my name is Maja, i'm a 24 year old dane who currently live in Ringsted. I come from a family of 5, where im the oldest out of 3 siblings. I have a slightly different cultural backgroun, as my father comes from Libanon. I never learned how to speak arabic, we do however visit his side of the family as often as conflict allows. I decided to start on IT-Architekture as i think it aligns well with my personal interest.

I always have something to do when I get home, i'm quite good at busying myself. in my sparetime I love to read, listen to music, embroider, crochet, knit, draw, sewing, gaming and I volunteer. I volunteer for RUF (Ringsted Ungdoms Forum) where my main task is to set up event for people within the age of 18-25. This will be my second year doing this, and some of the assignments I'm currently working on are a sushi event and a D&D event. The premise for what we do is to create comunities and spaces for people who might have a hard time socializing or just moved to the city without having a base. I quite enjoy the work, albeit a lot of the other member are around 4-5 years younger than I.

When i read i mainly read high fantasy, and my alltime favorite book series is Eragon by Christopher Paolini. I have always loved the idea of magic and dragons. I grew up with folklore as my bedtime stories, and have always been encouraged by those around me to let my imagination run wild. I collect book series that i enjoy and me and my roommate are around 5 books away from being able to call our collection a library(for it to classify as a library it most contain 1000+ books). This year alone i have read 25 new books, and i'm currently working throug Priory of the Orange tree.

Music is with me pretty much all day. I always have my headset with me, and i almost always have my headset on. I listen to a plethora of different artist, but some of the artist i listen to the most are Sleep Token, Ed Sheeran and Bad Omens. I use music as a tool. Sometimes the world can get to loud, so i use the music and my headset as a barrier that often helps me keep focus.

I grew up with arts and crafts. I'ts something my mothers side of the family cared a lot about, so i started learning the different art pretty early on. Anything from weaving to sewing really. I have a lot of really good memories from my childhood, where i would spend weekends or afternoons after school with my granma where we would sit with a good cup of tea and some project we were working on.

I started playing games pretty early on. I started with Playstation and Nintendo, and as i got older i gravitated towards pc. My friend introduced me League of Legends and Warframe, and i have been playing ever since. At the moment mostly League of Legends, Dead by Daylight, Peak, Lethal Company, Repo and Sea of thieves. I mostly play co-op games and i usually do so with some of my friends of England and Austria.
